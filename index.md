# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 16, 2026

Sergey Voronin ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application, Latera ("the App"), handles your data. The App is designed as a local-first application to ensure your personal files and data remain secure on your device.

## 1. Local Data Processing
Latera is an offline-first desktop application designed to index and search your local files. 
- **File Indexing:** All file scanning, text extraction, optical character recognition (OCR), and semantic index generation are performed entirely locally on your device. 
- **No Data Uploads:** The App does not upload, copy, transmit, or share your personal files, their contents, metadata, file names, or your search queries to our servers, the cloud, or any third party. Your search index (including SQLite databases and vector embeddings) is stored strictly on your local machine.

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect
We do not collect, store, or process any personal data, usage telemetry, or analytics (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or AppCenter). Your usage of the app remains completely private to you.

## 3. Network and Internet Usage
While Latera operates primarily offline, the App uses your device's internet connection strictly for the following purposes:
- **Downloading AI Models:** To enable semantic search and AI features, the App downloads necessary Open Neural Network Exchange (ONNX) models (such as large language models) directly from public repositories (e.g., Hugging Face) to your device.
- **License Verification:** If you purchase or activate a Pro version, the App communicates securely with the Microsoft Store or our licensing service solely to verify your purchase status and unlock extended features. No personal files or search queries are sent during this process.

## 4. Crash Reports
Currently, we do not automatically collect crash reports or diagnostic data. If the App crashes or experiences an error, no automatic network requests are made to send debug information to us. If you encounter an issue, we encourage you to contact us directly to provide feedback. 

*(Note: If we decide to implement automated, opt-in crash reporting in the future to improve the application's stability, we will update this Privacy Policy and explicitly request your consent before any error logs are transmitted).*

## 5. Security of Your Information
Because your files and search index never leave your device, the security of this data relies entirely on your local operating system's security and your physical device protections. 

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our App does not purposefully collect or solicit any information from anyone under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal data from any user, no data from children is stored or transmitted by our App.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy or by displaying a prominent notice within the App.
